Kabuly Pulao

Ingredienrs:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb, cubed
  • 1 large onion, seliced
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 2 carrots, julinned
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • salt, black pepper, cumin, cardamom

Cooking Instructions:

  1. soak the rice in wather for 30 minutes and drain.
  2. Fry onions until golden brow.
  3. Add lamb and cook until browned season with spiced.
  4. Add Wather and simmer until the meat is tender.
  5. In a separate pan, fry carrots, raisins and almonds lightly
  6. Boil the rice until half cooked, then draim.
  7. Lyer meat and rice in a pot. top with carrot mixture.
  8. Cover and steam on low heat for 20-30 minutes.

Shir Yakh (Afghan Ice Cream)

Descrition:A creamy and refreshing frozen dessert that made with milk, sugar and cardemom a beloved Afhan treat during hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardemom powder
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)
  • Crushed ice or freezer for chiling

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk and sugar together until slightly thickened.
  2. Add cardemom and rose water.
  3. Let the mixture cool.
  4. pour into small small cups and freeze for 4-6 hours
  5. serve cold , optionally topped with crushed pistachios or nuts. /li>

Noodel

Description

A quick and tasty dish made with boiled noodles, vegetales and spices . Great for lunch or dinner when you're short on time!

Ingredients

  • 200g noodels
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black papper
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodels according to package Instructions and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saute vegetables until soft.
  3. Add cooked noodels, soy sauce, salt and papper.
  4. Mix well and cook for 2-3 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy
